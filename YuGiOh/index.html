MONSTER CARDS
YUGIOH! Monster Cards are the primary cards used in battles. They represent creatures with unique stats, attributes, and effects. Each monster has an Attack (ATK) and Defense (DEF) value, along with a Level (★) or Rank (Xyz) / Link Rating (Link Monsters). Monsters can be Normal, Effect, Fusion, Synchro, Xyz, Pendulum, or Link types, each with different summoning conditions and abilities. Some monsters have powerful effects that can change the game, while others serve as material for special summons.

TRAP CARDS
YUGIOH! Trap Cards are cards that are set face-down and activated in response to an opponent's action or during specific phases. They provide various effects, such as negating attacks, disrupting plays, or boosting your own strategy. There are three main types: Normal Trap – One-time use, then sent to the Graveyard. Continuous Trap – Stays on the field and has ongoing effects. Counter Trap – The fastest type, used to negate effects or actions. Trap Cards are key to defense and strategic counterplay in duels.
